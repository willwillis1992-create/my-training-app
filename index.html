Sunday prep ~1 hour

Covers 5 lunches, 5 breakfasts & snacks. Steps run in parallel.

0:00
Roast veg (passive)
Sheet-pan veg (courgette, peppers, tomatoes) at 200°C, 25 min. Cooks itself while you do the rest.
0:10
Ground beef + sweet potatoes
Brown 500g beef with garlic, paprika, cumin, salt. Cube & roast sweet potatoes at 200°C 25 min alongside veg.
0:35
Assemble 5 lunch boxes
200g sweet potato + 100g beef + veg per box. Small dressing pot on side. Fridge 4 days, freeze 1.
0:45
Overnight oats × 5
5 jars: 80g oats + 200ml oat milk + 1 scoop whey + chia seeds. Add banana fresh each morning.
0:55
Pack snacks
5 × 30g nut bags (almonds/pistachios). 5 × 200g Greek yoghurt tubs. Done.
